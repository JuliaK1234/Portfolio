My Hero

Jessica

But that's not all, she always encourages me to speak up toward others and understands my pains better than anyone. I look to her for advice and aspire to be like her. She always explains a lot of things to me and has good speaking skills despite being an introvert. What's funny is that I'm more extroverted than her, but my speaking skills are bad.
